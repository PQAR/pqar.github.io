Nuclear Reactor

Our team is actively involved in the development of advanced nuclear reactor (light water) designs with enhanced safety features and efficiency. We explore innovative reactor designs and materials, aiming to contribute to the sustainable production of clean energy.

Particle Accelerator

Researching and designing particle accelerators, we focus on accelerating particles to high energies for various scientific experiments. These accelerators play a crucial role in studying fundamental particles, enabling discoveries that advance our understanding of the universe.

Operating System for Research

We are developing a specialized operating system tailored for scientific research purposes. This operating system provides a stable and secure environment for running complex simulations, computations, and experiments, facilitating seamless scientific exploration.

Programming Language for Research

Creating a domain-specific programming language optimized for research applications, our team aims to simplify and enhance the implementation of scientific algorithms. This language is designed to be efficient, user-friendly, and versatile, catering specifically to the needs of researchers in various fields.
